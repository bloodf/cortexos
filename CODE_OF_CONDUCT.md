# Code of Conduct

> Contributor Covenant 2.1 adaptation for CortexOS community spaces.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in CortexOS welcoming, safe, and productive for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Use respectful, professional language.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what benefits project and users.
- Show empathy toward different experience levels.
- Keep disagreements technical, specific, and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized comments or imagery in project spaces.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or support channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, block users, or report abuse when behavior violates this code. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented, and focused on restoring safe collaboration.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to project maintainers through private channel when available. Include relevant links, screenshots, timeline, and requested outcome. Maintainers will acknowledge reports, review evidence, and respond with appropriate action.

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, community chat, events, and any public representation of CortexOS.
